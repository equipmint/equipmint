How EquipMint works

Three quick steps from loadout idea to physical keychain.

Step 1
Choose your case

Select one of five shells – from stealth black to neon frost. Cases are reused forever; you only swap the cores.

Step 2
Pick 2 cores

Each core is a knife or glove design. Mix any two: double knives, double gloves, or one of each.

Step 3
Swap when your mains change

Day-trade your taste. Pop the case open, slide out a core, and drop in a new one when you switch skins.
